Welcome to the official SpeedcubeConference website!

What is Speedcube Conference?

Speedcube Conference is a weekly event where speedcubers can come together to compete and talk about the cubing world!

How can I sign up?

You can sign up here!

What are the rules?

Here are the rules:

  • The rules of the WCA apply. Find them here.
  • Please be sure to keep up to the schedule! I'll give the schedule for each week seperately.
  • If you want to compete, please bring your own cube.
  • If you just want to spectate, be sure to watch in a manner where you don't disturb any other parts of the event.
  • Enjoy!
